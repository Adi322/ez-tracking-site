Product Overview

How EZ Tracking works and what makes it powerful.

How It Works

Drivers will input the customer name, email, phone number, order number, description, and delivery address into our app.

The app generates a live tracking link and sends it to the customer email.

Customers can view real-time location updates on the map.

Drivers can join restuarants to start delivering by first installing the app and then getting sent an invite link to their email so they join your restaurant.

Key Features

Track any deliverable — from food to parcels — in real time.

Live ETAs help customers know exactly when their order will arrive.

Invite drivers via shareable URL — no install needed.

Customize your delivery marker on the map.
